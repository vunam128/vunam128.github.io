Dr. Nam is an Assistant Professor of Applied Artificial Intelligence at the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence, Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology (PTIT), Viet Nam. He holds distinguished roles as a NVIDIA University Ambassador and a VINIF Fellow, highlighting his recognized expertise and contributions in the field. Dr. Nam received B.Sc. in Telecommunications (2013) from Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST), where he graduated as the Valedictorian of his cohort, Master's degree in Computer Science (2015) from Chonnam National University, South Korea, and Ph.D. in Computer Engineering (2024) from the Posts and Telecommunications Institute of Technology (PTIT).

His main research direction focuses on sign language recognition and generation models, vision-language models for recognition in the fields of agriculture, healthcare, and education. Additionally, He also develop Vision systems for UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) geared towards Edge AI-based UAV applications.
